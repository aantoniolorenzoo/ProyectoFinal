About us

Buy fresh fish and seafood at home. Coming mostly from the coasts of the Mediterranean. Among our products, the best seafood at home stands out: prawns from Vinaròs and seafood from the Ebre Delta, such as rock mussels (famous for their flavor), tellinas, oysters, razor clams, blue crab, clams. All the products in our online store are caught through selective artisanal fishing, which respects the marine environment.
